Welcome to the Madison Snow Mob

Madison gets a few great snowstorms each winter. Let's have some fun with them by building big snow sculptures. It's a community thing.

Join by sending a text to 608-729-7890. That's all you have to do.

How it works
  1. You join (by sending a text)
  2. You submit ideas for sculptures
  3. The group votes on what to make the next snowstorm
  4. We choose a place
  5. SnowMob will send a text to everyone of the time & place for the next snowmob
  6. We all show up with shovels, snow molds, energy, smiles, ideas, plans, goodwill, perhaps some food & drink...
  7. We build an epically cool snow sculpture
  8. We have fun
